# Contributing

OpenPhone accepts external contributions from contributors who agree to the
project contributor terms.

Before submitting code, documentation, patches, designs, schemas, or other
materials, contributors must agree in writing that:

- they have the right to submit the contribution,
- the contribution is assigned to OpenPhone / the project company, or is
  otherwise licensed broadly enough for OpenPhone to own, modify, sublicense,
  and redistribute it,
- the contribution may be distributed under the OpenPhone public
  non-commercial license,
- the contribution may also be distributed under separate OpenPhone commercial
  licenses, and
- the contribution does not knowingly include third-party material that
  OpenPhone does not have the right to use.

Pull requests may be reviewed before the formal contributor agreement is
complete, but OpenPhone-owned code will not be merged until the contributor
terms are accepted.

Do not submit third-party proprietary code, vendor blobs, Google apps, Google
Mobile Services, or other restricted materials to this repository.

GPL-covered contributions must remain under the GPL and must satisfy GPL source
obligations. OpenPhone does not add non-commercial restrictions to GPL-covered
materials.
